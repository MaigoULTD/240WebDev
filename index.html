BSIT

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) Program is a joint program provided by the College of Management(CM) and the College of Science and Mathematics(CSM). The program provides a quality education that focuses on the skills and knowledge needed by one of the fastest growing fields.

If you pursue a BS in information technology (IT), you will develop a deep and wide understanding of a range of topics, issues, and applications that face leaders in today’s technologically advancing world. This major is jointly sponsored by CM and UMass Boston’s College of Science and Mathematics. You will develop a foundation of programming, networking, and web-based competencies.

As an IT major, you then go on to specialize in business intelligence, computer forensics, information architecture, or system administration, all of which provide real work examples and experience in IT problemsolving. You will further complement your knowledge of information technology with professional electives designed to expand your understanding of important management or programming issues to further prepare you for your specific career path.

Students come to CM from across the United States and abroad to benefit from our dedication to student success. We are committed to providing you with the individual attention and support needed to make your goals a reality. The diversity and cosmopolitan nature of the student body add another valuable dimension to the overall experience.

We will help you build a firm and broad foundation for your studies by introducing you to all of the functional areas of business leadership and management. You will develop strong business communication skills, master quantitative reasoning techniques, and participate in valuable group work through case-study and real-world opportunities. Exposure to these essential skills and knowledge areas will also prepare you to choose one of the concentrations below.
